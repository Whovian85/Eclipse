ABOUT US


VISIT AB INBEV

It’s simple: brewing and enjoying great beer depends on healthy environments and prosperous communities. As brewers, we thrive when our farmers, suppliers, partners and consumers thrive. Sustainability isn’t just part of our business: it is our business.

But every day seems to introduce new challenges to our communities and business. From climate change, to natural disasters, and economic instability, the problems are bigger than any of us can solve alone.

That’s why we’re introducing Eclipse. Inspired by those cosmic moments when stars and planets align, Eclipse is a new sustainability-dedicated collaboration platform developed for our network of suppliers and partners. It facilitates shared goals and target-setting, standardized ways of measurement, and best practice communication around the SDG areas most critical to our shared future.

Eclipse is our commitment to a better future, together. And together, we can lead the change.
